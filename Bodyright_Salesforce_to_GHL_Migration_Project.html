1. Project Overview

Why Switching from Salesforce to GHL?

Salesforce Problems

  • Costs $5,000/month - massively overpriced
  • Enterprise version sold unnecessarily (less than 2,000 contacts)
  • Very clunky and not user-friendly
  • Pardot used ONLY for monthly newsletter - waste of money
  • No integration between Salesforce and PracSuite
  • Difficult to send bulk emails or newsletters
  • Limited AI capabilities (would cost ~$20K extra)
  • Poor support from Salesforce

GHL Benefits

  • Fraction of the cost of Salesforce
  • Right-sized for Bodyright's needs
  • User-friendly interface
  • Built-in forms, landing pages, email, SMS, calendar
  • API/Zapier integration with PracSuite possible
  • Easy newsletter sending
  • AI capabilities built-in (Phase 2)
  • No per-user cost increases

Project Goal

Migrate all CRM, automation, lead management, referral tracking, email marketing, and nurturing workflows from Salesforce / Pardot / JotForm / ActiveCampaign into GoHighLevel (GHL) while maintaining integration with PracSuite (practice management software) and PandaDoc (digital signatures).
